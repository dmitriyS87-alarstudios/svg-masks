What Are Digital Therapeutics?
Digital therapeutics are a new types of digital health software intervention. These web applications are designed to help treat particular diseases or conditions and are private, secure and easy to use.
Because of COVID, the world is confronting a mental health crisis of unimaginable proportions.

There’s a growing gap between the number of people who need care, and the amount of providers available to deliver it. Ensemble helps provide you with the support to give your patients the best care possible.

Patients diagnosed with MDD or GAD may be eligible to participate in virtual a research study of Ensemble. This research study is designed to test the safety and efficacy of this investigational medical device. Participants will be compensated for their time.

During the study participants will complete two short, engaging activities right from a phone or computer for a total of 15-30 minutes per day. Through participationg, they’ll learn skills that may help them gain better control over their anxiety or depression.

Ensemble will be provided at no cost to participants and they can withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.
