How it Works

Ensemble delivers skills training based on CBT, mindfulness and positive psychology.

Activities are thoughtfully sequenced to achieve the desired outcome.

Measures patient mental health improvement and outcomes using clinically validated instruments (PHQ-9 and GAD-7).

Facilitates connection to immediate resources if patient reports thinking about self harm; you'll be notified as well.

Includes a physician dashboard for patient monitoring.

Uses the science of engagement to increase patient adherence.
